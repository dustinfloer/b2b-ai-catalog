Fresh directions for SE Field Guide

Three SVG concepts built around the new product direction: trusted navigation, field craft, and a decision layer for SEs. Wayfinder is the selected direction because it says "find the right path" without feeling like a generic documentation site.

Wayfinder

Selected

A compass/path mark for orientation and decision-making. This fits the Field Guide direction best: not a folder of assets, but a trusted path through SE work.

Best for the homepage and favicon direction.

Field Notes

A manual/notebook mark. This feels more personal and artifact-like, which supports the imprint goal, but it reads slightly more "documentation" than "operating layer."

Best if the guide should feel like a durable handbook.

Signal Stack

Stacked cards with a highlighted route. This is the most operational option and maps well to "find, decide, execute," but it is less distinctive than Wayfinder.

Best if the product becomes more dashboard-like.
