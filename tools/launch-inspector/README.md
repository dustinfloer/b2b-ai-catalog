## What It Does

Monitor merchant launch progress across DTC and B2B from a single interface. Queries Salesforce launch cases, checks live store data via BigQuery, compares against revenue targets, and surfaces actionable recommendations — without needing to jump between tools.

## When to Use It

- During post-sales to track where a merchant stands against their launch milestones
- When you need a quick health check on a B2B or DTC launch before a check-in call
- To identify blockers or gaps between current store data and revenue targets

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Launch Inspector](https://launch-inspector.quick.shopify.io/)
2. Search for the merchant by name or Salesforce case
3. Review launch status, store data, and recommendations
