## What It Does

Generates tailored discovery questions and merchant research to prepare SEs for discovery calls. Helps ensure you ask the right questions based on the merchant's industry, size, and known pain points.

## When to Use It

- Before initial discovery calls with new prospects
- When preparing for follow-up discovery to go deeper on specific topics
- To identify gaps in your discovery and plan next questions

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Discovery Prep](https://discovery-prep.quick.shopify.io/)
2. Input the merchant details and context
3. Review the generated discovery plan and questions
