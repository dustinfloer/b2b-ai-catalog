## What It Does

Pulls merchant context from Salesforce, discovery notes, and prior call history to generate a pre-populated demo preparation sheet. Helps SEs walk into every demo with structured context on the merchant's business, pain points, and technical requirements.

## When to Use It

- Before any merchant-facing demo or technical call
- When picking up a deal from another SE and need quick context
- To ensure consistent, thorough prep across the team

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Demo Prep](https://se-demo-prep.quick.shopify.io/)
2. Enter the merchant name or Salesforce Opportunity details
3. Review the auto-populated fields and adjust as needed
