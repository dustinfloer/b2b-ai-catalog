## What It Does

Pulls activity from your active deals (emails, Slack, etc.) and generates a concise summary covering current state, next steps, and PMF/TA status. The summary is delivered to you as a DM where you can review, edit, approve, or send.

## When to Use It

- Before weekly pipeline reviews when you need a quick state-of-the-deal across active opportunities
- For monthly stakeholder updates or AE syncs without manually rebuilding the narrative each time
- When you want recurring deal updates auto-generated and sent on a schedule (weekly, monthly, etc.)

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Weekly Deal Update](https://skills.quick.shopify.io/#/weekly-deal-update)
2. Configure which deals to track and your preferred cadence
3. Review the DM you receive, edit if needed, then approve or auto-send
