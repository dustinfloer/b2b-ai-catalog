04 · B2B Accounts

Companies, locations, and contract pricing — built for B2B.

A hospital system with four locations. A dental DSO with fifteen practices. A vet chain with regional hubs. Shopify's company model maps directly to how healthcare buyers are organized.

  • ✓ One company, unlimited ship-to locations
  • ✓ Role-based permissions (buyer, approver, admin)
  • ✓ Per-location payment terms and price lists
  • ✓ Consolidated reporting across the whole company
Your GO (Group/Contract) pricing model lives directly in this hierarchy — each company gets their negotiated catalog and terms, every location under them inherits.
04 · B2B Accounts

Tiered contract pricing, without custom code.

Healthcare facilities get -25% and Net 30. Small practices and dental get -10% with due-on-fulfillment terms. All managed in the admin, synced from D365, visible the moment a buyer logs in.

  • ✓ Fixed prices, % adjustments, or volume-break pricing
  • ✓ Catalog scoping — show only the products each tier can buy
  • ✓ Pricing sync from D365 via partner-built integration
  • ✓ EDI orders don't count toward GMV thresholds (preserves margin)
Your contract pricing lives in D365 today. With Shopify, those prices flow directly into price lists — every buyer always sees their exact negotiated price.
