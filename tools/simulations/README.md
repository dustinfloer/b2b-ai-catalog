## What It Does

A collection of interactive simulations designed for B2B sales scenarios. Use these to walk merchants through realistic product experiences and demonstrate Shopify capabilities in context.

## When to Use It

- During live demos to show merchants realistic scenarios
- For prospect enablement and self-guided exploration
- Team training and onboarding on B2B selling motions

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Simulations](https://simulations.quick.shopify.io/)
2. Select the simulation that matches your scenario
3. Walk through the interactive experience with your merchant
