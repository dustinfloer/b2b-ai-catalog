# AMER B2B Partner LookBook

Internal Google Sheet for AMER B2B partner discovery and qualification. Use it when an SE needs to identify implementation partners with relevant B2B experience, vertical fit, platform migration background, co-sell expertise, published case studies, or Shopify-verified B2B skills.

## Coverage

- Partner ID, partner name, tier, and partner manager
- Website, target segment, and top verticals
- Complementary technology partner experience
- Competitor migration experience
- B2B customer examples and closed-won context
- Co-sell expertise, case studies, verified skills, accelerators, and outbound readiness

## When to Use It

- Matching a merchant to potential B2B implementation partners
- Preparing for partner-led or co-sell solutioning conversations
- Checking which partners have relevant vertical, migration, or enterprise B2B experience
- Finding internal partner spotlight recordings or co-marketing context
