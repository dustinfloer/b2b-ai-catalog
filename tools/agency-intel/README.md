## What It Does

Provides research and intelligence on Shopify agency partners to help SEs recommend the right implementation partner for merchants. Surface agency capabilities, industry specializations, and relevant experience.

## When to Use It

- When a merchant needs an implementation partner recommendation
- During deal scoping to understand available agency support
- To research agency capabilities for specific technical requirements

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [Agency Intel](https://agency-intel.quick.shopify.io/)
2. Search or browse agencies by specialization, industry, or capability
3. Review agency profiles and match to your merchant's needs
